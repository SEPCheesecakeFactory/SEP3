# User Guide

## Learner Guide

### Register an account

1. Click on Login

![Starting page](image-6.png)

2. Go to Register

![Login click on register](image-7.png)

3. Complete the fields and register the new user.

![Register a  user](image-8.png)

### Login

1. Click on Login

![Starting Page](image-9.png)

2. Complete the fields and log in with your user.

![Login](image-10.png)

### See my courses

1. Click on My Courses in the NavMenu.

![My Courses](image-11.png)

### Continue Learning from started course

[Starting point, end of See my courses]

1. Click on the course desired to resume.

![Resume Course](image-12.png)

### See All Courses

1. Click on All Courses in the NavMenu.

![All Courses](image-13.png)

### Filter Courses

[Starting point, end of See All Courses]

There are two different options for filtering, by course name and by course category.

![Filtering Options](image-14.png)

#### Filter by name, which shows only the courses containing the desired characters

1. Click on the search bar and write the desired text.

![Search by Course Name](image-15.png)

#### Filter by category

1. Click on All Categories and select the desired one.

![Search by Category](image-16.png)

2. You will see all courses matching that category.

![Matching Courses](image-17.png)

### Unenroll from a course

[Starting point, end of See my courses]

1. Click on the bin icon in the top right corner of the course.

![Unenroll from Course](image-18.png)

### See Leaderboard

1. Click on the Leaderboard button on the NavMenu.

![Leaderboard](image-19.png)

## Teacher Guide

### Submit a Course Draft

[Starting point, then end of See All Courses]

1. Click on Create Draft button.

![Start Create Draft](image-20.png)

2. Complete fields and click on Create Draft.

![Create a Draft](image-44.png)

### See the courses done by your user

[Starting point, then end of See All Courses]

1. Click on My Workspace.

![See My courses](image-45.png)

### Manage Course Content

#### Modify an existing step

[Starting point, then end of See All Courses]

1. Click on the first icon from the top right corner on the desired course.

![Start Edit](image-38.png)

2. Complete fields and click on Save Changes.

![Edit Step information](image-40.png)

3. To see what will be displayed to the user, click on Back to Preview button.

![Preview Step](image-41.png)

#### Create a learning step

[Starting point, end of step 1 of Modify an existing step]

1. Click on Next until you see the screen to create a new learning step.

![Next Learning Step](image-39.png)

1. Click on Add New Step button.

![Add new learning step](image-42.png)

2. Complete the fields as desired and click on Save Changes button.

![Save Learning Step](image-43.png)

### Edit Course Information

1. Click on the pencil icon on the rop right corner of the desired course.

![Start Edit Course Information](image-36.png)

2. Modify the fields and click on Save.

![Edit Course Information](image-37.png)
